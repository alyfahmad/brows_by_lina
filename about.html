Hello! I'm Lina Monowar, Owner and Licensed Aesthetician at Brows and Beauty by Lina.
With over 15 years of experience from abroad and the US, my first priority is to provide you with excellent services that helps attain clear, healthy, & glowing skin through facial treatments; as well as attend to your grooming needs with a hair removal treatment designed for your skin, to ensure you won't hurt your skin but instead help it become even more beautiful. I believe in communicating with my clients to ensure they are satisfied with results that will last, and help you realistically attain your skin goals. I can finally help you attain your "Dream Brows" & "Dream Skin"! My services include Threading, Waxing, Eyebrow Tinting, Microblading, Facials, Henna, & Party Makeup/Lessons. Each of my services are customized to each clients needs and skin type. I ensure you not only look beautiful but feel beautiful and stay being beautiful! Your happiness & satisfaction is my 1st priority!
